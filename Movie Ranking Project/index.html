My Top 5 Movies of Tamil 2024

Vettiyan

Vettaiyan is an action-packed crime drama starring Rajinikanth as Athiyan, a supercop who gets caught in unexpected events after gunning down a criminal.The film explores themes of justice and morality, and it's available for streaming on Amazon Prime Video

The Greatest of All Time (GOAT)

GOAT - The Greatest of All Time features Vijay as a top hostage negotiator and spy whose past comes back to haunt him after retirement. The film is available on Netflix and offers a gripping storyline with intense action sequences

Kanguva

Kanguva is Suriya's epic fantasy drama where he plays dual roles as a tribal warrior and a contemporary cop. The film, directed by Siva, has been released in theaters and is expected to be available on Amazon Prime Video around Pongal 2025

Amaran

"Amaran" is a biographical action war film starring Sivakarthikeyan and Sai Pallavi2. It is based on the true-life story of Major Mukund Varadarajan, a commissioned officer in the Indian Army's Rajput Regiment, who was posthumously awarded the Ashok Chakra for his valor during a counterterrorism operation in Jammu and Kashmir

Raayan
